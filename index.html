About this project

The Mastercraft bamboo Monitor riser is a study and stylish platform that evelates your screen to a more comfortable viewing height. placing your monitor at eye level has the potential to improve your posture and make you more comfortable while at work, helping you stay focused on the task at hand.

Featuring artisan craftsmanship, the simplicity of design creates extra desk space below your computer to allow notepads, pens, and USB sticks to be stored under the stand.
